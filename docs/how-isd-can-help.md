---

## <iconify-icon icon="fluent-emoji-flat:compass" width="28" height="28" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 0.3em;"></iconify-icon> Strategic Planning and Assessment

- SOC assessments, including use of the SOF AI-Enabled SOC CMM.
- Current-state analysis of technology, processes, and workforce.

---

## <iconify-icon icon="fluent-emoji-flat:building-construction" width="28" height="28" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 0.3em;"></iconify-icon> Platform Design and Implementation

- Architecture, deployment, and integration of Microsoft Defender XDR, Microsoft Sentinel, Defender for Cloud, and Threat Intelligence.
- Migration from third-party SIEM/XDR solutions.

---

## <iconify-icon icon="fluent-emoji-flat:robot" width="28" height="28" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 0.3em;"></iconify-icon> Generative and Agentic AI Enablement

- Security Copilot planning, onboarding, and extensibility (promptbooks, plugins, automation patterns).
- AI risk management and governance design for SOC use cases.
- Early Agent 365 and SOC agent use-case design and pilots.

---

## <iconify-icon icon="fluent-emoji-flat:chart-increasing" width="28" height="28" style="vertical-align: middle; margin-right: 0.3em;"></iconify-icon> Operational Optimization

- SOC operating model design (staffing, tiers, MXDR integration).
- Automation and playbook engineering.
- Metrics, reporting, and continuous improvement practices.

---

{: .note }
> Organizations can use ISD to accelerate their SOC transformation, reduce risk when adopting new AI and agentic capabilities, and connect technology changes to measurable business and compliance outcomes.
