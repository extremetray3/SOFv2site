One year after publishing our "SOC of the Future" briefing (Jan 2025), many of its forward-looking predictions have rapidly become reality. The threat landscape has intensified, and Microsoft's security technology stack has evolved in parallel. Rather than a slow, stepwise journey from SOC I to SOC II to SOC III, we are seeing vertical integration of capabilities: unified XDR/SIEM, generative AI, and early agentic automation are maturing together.
